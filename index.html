About Us

The Heart of Suryapet's Celebrations

Rudrangi Convention Hall is Suryapet's most sought-after venue for weddings, receptions, corporate gatherings, and grand celebrations. Designed to accommodate events of all scales, our hall blends modern amenities with traditional grandeur.

From intimate family functions to lavish weddings with thousands of guests, we provide the perfect backdrop for your most cherished moments. Our dedicated team ensures every detail is handled with precision and care.

  • Premium decor and lighting systems
  • Spacious parking for 200+ vehicles
  • In-house catering with custom menus
  • Bridal suite and guest rooms
  • Air-conditioned banquet hall
  • Stage, sound system & LED screens
